Gamepad:
Left stick to move
Right stick can aim attacks
Up on d-pad to switch to slashing attacks
Down on d-pad to switch to bashing attacks
left face button for normal attacks
up face button special attacks
down face button for jumping
right face button for dodging

Keyboard + Mouse:
WASD to move
arrows to aim attacks
1 to switch to slashing attacks
2 to switch to bashing attacks
left click or q for normal attacks
right click or e for special attacks
space for jumping
tab for dodging

The player will automatically grab ledges when falling next to one.
Hold up + jump to climb up ledges after having grabbed them.
Jumping otherwise causes you to jump off the ledge
